Background

I have always had a passion and curiosity when it comes to technology. From an early age this manifested itself in ways like me taking apart the remote to see the insides, or trying to rebuild my toys after opening them. My parents weren't the biggest fans of my methods, but appreciated my inquisitiveness. After high school I explored this further by attending SAE Insititute in NYC for Audio Engineering. Soon after interning in studio's my thirst for the technical side of things increased and I began to 'Google-up' on the world of code. After teaching myself SQL online through "W3Schools", I then began to research bootcamp's to fully submerged myself into the world of code. Since making that wonderful decision I have learned how many ways I can affect the world as a developer. My drive to learn has never been higher and I look forward to my continued journey of knowledge.
